Welcome to the NoCaSS 2021 application form.

The application deadline for this meeting is 21th June 2021.

Everyone (even if you are giving a talk) is encouraged to submit a post-it note of their science. For this please draw/doodle a single aspect of your science on a post-it note. It should explicitly be only a single aspect. Please see this lovely example below.

Please submit this via email to JIC.NoCaSS@jic.ac.uk until the 16th June 2021. Please send it either as a jpeg or png file with a maximum size of 3MB. For further insiprations have a look at this twitter thread.

If you would like to be considered for a talk, please select the ‘talk’ option and submit your abstract by 11th June 2021. There will be a competition and prizes for the best talks.

Privacy Statement: The NoCaSS 2021 organising committee takes data privacy seriously and will only use the information you have provided for the organisation or follow up of the NoCaSS 2021 meeting. We may contact you directly before or after the meeting but will otherwise not store or use your data.

Summary of the deadlines 11th June if you want to be considered for a talk 16th June if you want to get a goody bag and/or submit a science post-it 21st June if you want to attend the meeting
