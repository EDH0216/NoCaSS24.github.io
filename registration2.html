Welcome to the NoCaSS 2020 application form.

The application deadline for this meeting is 11th May 2020. Anyone wishing to present a poster can do so by selecting ‘poster’ and submitting an abstract with their registration before this deadline.

If you would like to be considered for a talk, please select the ‘talk’ option and submit your abstract by 15th April 2020. Anyone not selected for a talk, will automatically be given a poster presentation slot.

Privacy Statement:

The NoCaSS 2020 organising committee takes data privacy seriously and will only use the information you have provided for the organisation or follow up of the NoCaSS 2020 meeting. We may contact you directly before or after the meeting but will otherwise not store or use your data.
