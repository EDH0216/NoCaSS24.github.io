Sponsorship

NoCaSS 2014 will attract 100 PhD students in the Plant Sciences from the leading academic institutions in the East of England: the universities of Cambridge and East Anglia. Sponsors have a rare opportunity to connect with these future leaders in Plant Science.

We have several pre-defined sponsorship packages available below. To discuss these packages, please contact Jeremy Solly (jes200@cam.ac.uk).

Sponsor prizes for posters

You get:
  • Host a stall during lunch.
  • Have your company name and logo featured on the website and posters.
  • Present the poster prizes.

Sponsor prizes for talks

You get:
  • Host a stall during lunch.
  • Have your company name and logo featured on the website and posters.
  • Present the talk prizes.
  • Attend the evening drinks reception.

Sponsor the evening drinks reception

You get:
  • Host a stall during lunch.
  • Have your company name and logo featured on the website and posters.
  • Give a short presentation after lunch.
  • Attend and optionall host a stall at the drinks reception.
  • Attend the delegates dinner.
