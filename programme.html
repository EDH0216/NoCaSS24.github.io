Programme of NoCaSS 2021 will be available here

NoCaSS2021 will be in the afternoon of the 23rd June.

The programme is yet to be finalised, but will probably be 1pm to 4pm, with an open end social session from then onwards. If you register in a timely manner we will do our best to get you a goody bag for this session :)

We will be using zoom for the talks, and gather.town for the social session.

Talks will be 5 min. long and we would like you to focus on an indiviudal aspect of your research. That could either be a single experiment or result or a singular approach to a question.

We will turn on zoom's automatic subtitle caption function. You will be able to turn the subtitles on and off for yourself as an option in the lower option bar on zoom. If there is something we can do to improve your personal accessibility please do send us an eamil at JIC.NoCaSS@jic.ac.uk.

Sandy Knapp from the Natural History Museum in London will join us for a talk!
