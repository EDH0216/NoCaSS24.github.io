NoCaSS 2015 is being hosted at the John Innes Centre in Norwich

Location

The John Innes Centre is located to the east of Norwich, next to the Norfolk & Norwich University Hospital.

Transport

Transport links from Cambridge to Norwich are good, with frequent trains between the two cities. Taxis from the train station to the John Innes Centre should take approximately 20 mins and cost £6.

For those driving, the A11 goes almost directly from Cambridge to Norwich, with the journey being approximately 1 hour 30 mins.
